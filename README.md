# SSDS 2018  - 3<sup>nd</sup> Int'l Summer School on Data Science

## Center of Research Excellence for Data Science and Advanced Cooperative Systems, Research Unit for Data Science

Hands-on sessions for [3nd International Summer School on Data Science](https://sites.google.com/site/ssdatascience2018/) organized by the Center of Research Excellence for Data Science and Advanced Cooperative Systems, [Research Unit for Data Science](http://across-datascience.zci.hr/zci/istrazivanje/znanost_o_podatcima), from September 24-28, 2018 in Split, Croatia.
